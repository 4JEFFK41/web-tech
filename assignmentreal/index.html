Southampton Student Housing

We are a new business that was formed after the COVID-19 pandemic dedicated to help students find their new home
as smoothly as possible, we understand how difficult moving into a rented property can be and are here to make sure
the students are worry free, we deal with many issues such as:

  • Finding the perfect property with certain specifications
  • Dealing with estate agents/landlords to get the best price possible
  • Providing a summary of expected bills per month
  • Setting up necessities such as WiFi
  • Keeping in contact with estate agents/landlords for jobs that need doing
  • And much more...

We charge a fixed rate and prices are dependant on the property and will be calculated after you have applied, click Apply Now to get a quote.
